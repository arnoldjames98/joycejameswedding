Our story

Joyce and James met at Arizona State University in July of 2019. They were both hired as Residential Assistants and had the luck of being on the same floors of adjacent buildings connected by a bridge. They were instant friends as they shared the same humor and similar interests. Over time, their friendship and feelings grew and in November of 2019, they called it official.

Our story... continued

In 2020, James and Joyce were the only other people in each other’s COVID bubbles, apart from their families. Dating during the pandemic had its challenges but also brought them closer together.

James graduated from his master’s program at ASU in May of 2021. Following this, he moved to Cambridge, MA to pursue his Ph.D. in Computer Science at Harvard. Joyce and James did long-distance for 1 year. During this time, Joyce completed her undergraduate degree in Biochemistry at ASU.

In August of 2022, Joyce moved to join James in Cambridge, MA. Joyce works as a Montessori teacher at Wild Rose Montessori in a classroom of 3-6 year olds. She will be serving as Lead Guide and Co-Head of School in the coming fall.

James proposed to Joyce on November 5th, 2022. Over the course of their relationship, James and Joyce would go on all-day dates of back-to-back activities coined “all-daytes.” James planned a special all-dayte for their 3rd year anniversary which ended in a proposal in Cambridge.

After 5 years of making incredible memories and growing closer together, James and Joyce can’t wait to tie the knot and celebrate with their closest friends and family.
